## International Student Housing Challenges (Detailed)
Source: Bottom Line Property Management, April 24, 2025
URL: https://www.rentbottomline.com/blog/international-students-and-housing-challenges-solutions-and-future-directions

### Key Pain Points
1. **Financial Constraints**: Higher fees, currency exchange issues, limited income opportunities
2. **Legal/Documentation Barriers**: Lack of credit history, SSN, or guarantors
3. **Scam Exposure**: Fake listings, excessive deposits, discriminatory practices
4. **Language/Cultural Barriers**: Unfamiliar legal terms, cultural norms
5. **High Demand/Limited Supply**: Long waiting lists, inflated prices
6. **Discrimination**: Rejection based on nationality, race, immigration status

### Impact on Well-being
- Academic performance affected by housing instability
- Mental health issues: anxiety, depression, homesickness
- Social isolation in off-campus housing
- Physical safety concerns in unsafe neighborhoods

## Competitor Analysis

### Zillow
Source: Zillow Help Center, Nov 2025
- **SSN Requirement**: Can submit without SSN, but NO background check possible
- **Credit Check**: Required for most landlords (600+ score typically needed)
- **Limitation**: International students cannot get proper screening without SSN
- URL: https://zillow.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/360000949627

### KSL Homes (Utah-specific)
Source: KSL Homes Blog, Jan 2025
- **Tenant Screening**: $50 fee for credit and background check
- **Requirements**: Credit check, background check, previous landlord info
- **Limitation**: Requires US credit history
- URL: https://homes.ksl.com/blog/how-does-tenant-screening-work-on-a-ksl-homes-rent-listing

### CSSA WeChat Groups (Chinese Student Association)
- **Pros**: Chinese language, peer recommendations, cultural understanding
- **Cons**: Informal, no verification, scam risk, limited to Chinese community
- **No protection**: No formal verification or dispute resolution
