## What belongs here

The BridgeStay Landlord Partner Agreement governs the relationship between
BridgeStay LLC and verified landlords who list properties on the platform.

## Key sections to cover

- Landlord eligibility and verification requirements
- Listing standards (accurate photos, bilingual descriptions, honest pricing)
- BridgeStay's right to review, approve, or remove listings
- Commission or fee structure (if applicable)
- Landlord obligations to international tenants
- Non-discrimination policy (international students are a protected class
  under Fair Housing in many contexts)
- Data sharing and tenant privacy
- Termination of the landlord partnership
- Dispute resolution

## Suggested filename

`BridgeStay_Landlord_Agreement_v1.0.md` (draft)
`BridgeStay_Landlord_Agreement_v1.0.pdf` (signed copy — store locally, not in repo)

## Notes

- Signed landlord agreements should **never** be committed to version control
  (they contain PII). Store signed copies in `company/BridgeStay LLC/agreements/`.
- This folder holds the template only
- Consult a Utah business attorney before sending to landlords
