## What belongs here

The BridgeStay Privacy Policy explains how the platform collects, uses, stores,
and protects personal data — with special attention to international student data.

## Key sections to cover

- What data is collected (name, email, passport number, I-20, payment info)
- How data is used (identity verification, listing matching, payment processing)
- Third-party services (Supabase, Stripe, AWS S3, OpenAI)
- Data retention and deletion policy
- International data transfers (students from China, other countries)
- User rights (access, correction, deletion)
- Cookie policy
- Contact information for privacy requests

## Compliance considerations

- **FERPA** — if any integration with university systems is planned
- **CCPA** — California residents (applies even if HQ is Utah)
- **GDPR** — if any EU-resident students are served
- Sensitive data: passport numbers and I-20 documents require elevated protection
