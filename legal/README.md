# BridgeStay — Legal Documents

This folder contains **platform-facing legal documents** — agreements, policies,
and templates that govern the BridgeStay platform and its users.

> **Note:** This folder is for platform-facing documents only.
> Official company formation, tax, and filing records belong under
> `company/BridgeStay LLC/` and are **never committed to version control**.

---

## Folder Structure

| Folder | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `terms-of-service/` | Platform Terms of Service for landlords and tenants |
| `privacy-policy/` | Privacy Policy covering data collection and international student data |
| `lease-addendum/` | Lease addendum templates replacing SSN requirements with passport/I-20 verification |
| `landlord-agreement/` | Verified landlord partner agreements |

---

## Status

All subfolders are currently placeholders. Documents to be drafted or placed here
