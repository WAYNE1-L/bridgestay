# BridgeStay: 2-Minute Pitch Video Script

**Tim Draper Utah Entrepreneur Challenge (UEC) 2026**

**Total Runtime:** 2:00  
**Speaker:** Wayne Liang, Founder

---

## OPENING HOOK (0:00 - 0:15)

**[SCENE: Dark screen fades into a young student sitting alone in an airport terminal at night, looking at their phone with a worried expression. Soft, melancholic music plays.]**

**WAYNE (V.O.):**
> "Two years ago, I was sitting in Beijing Capital Airport at 2 AM, refreshing my email every thirty seconds. My flight to Salt Lake City left in six hours... and I still didn't have a place to live."

**[SCENE: Close-up of phone screen showing "Application Rejected" emails from Zillow and KSL.]**

**WAYNE (V.O.):**
> "Every landlord said the same thing: 'Sorry, we need a Social Security Number.' I didn't have one. I was an international student. And apparently, that made me invisible."

---

## THE PROBLEM (0:15 - 0:45)

**[SCENE: Cut to Wayne speaking directly to camera in a modern, well-lit space. Confident but relatable tone.]**

**WAYNE:**
> "That night, I ended up booking an overpriced Airbnb for my first month in Utah. I paid $2,800 for a place I'd never seen, from a host I'd never met, in a city I'd never visited. I was one of the lucky ones."

**[SCENE: B-roll of diverse international students on UofU campus, studying, walking, laughing.]**

**WAYNE (V.O.):**
> "There are over 3,000 international students at the University of Utah right now. Every single one of them faced the same impossible choice: trust a stranger online, or don't come to America at all."

**[SCENE: Split screen showing Zillow and KSL Homes application pages with "SSN Required" highlighted.]**

**WAYNE (V.O.):**
> "Zillow requires a Social Security Number for background checks. KSL Homes requires U.S. credit history. These platforms weren't built for us. So we built something that was."

---

## THE SOLUTION - THE "AHA" MOMENT (0:45 - 1:15)

**[SCENE: BridgeStay logo animation. Warm, inviting color palette. Music shifts to upbeat, hopeful tone.]**

**WAYNE:**
> "This is BridgeStay. The first rental platform designed specifically for international students."

**[SCENE: Screen recording of BridgeStay platform - showing passport/I-20 upload, verified listings, bilingual interface.]**

**WAYNE (V.O.):**
> "No SSN? No problem. Upload your passport and I-20 visa document, and you're verified in under 24 hours. Every listing on our platform is admin-verified—no scams, no fake photos, no surprises."

**[SCENE: Show the roommate matching feature and Chinese language toggle.]**

**WAYNE (V.O.):**
> "We built AI-powered roommate matching so students can find compatible housemates before they even land. And for our Chinese students—who make up the largest international demographic—the entire platform works in Mandarin."

**[SCENE: Wayne back on camera, gesturing enthusiastically.]**

**WAYNE:**
> "In just three months, we've matched over 150 students with verified housing. Our satisfaction rate is 98%. And we're just getting started."

---

## TRACTION & MARKET (1:15 - 1:35)

**[SCENE: Animated infographic showing key metrics.]**

**WAYNE (V.O.):**
> "The U.S. student housing market is worth $24 billion and growing 8% annually. International students alone contribute $44 billion to the American economy every year. Yet no major platform serves their unique needs."

**[SCENE: Map of Utah highlighting UofU, BYU, Utah Tech with student numbers.]**

**WAYNE (V.O.):**
> "We're starting right here in Utah—with 5,000 international students across three universities. But our vision is national. Every international student in America deserves a safe place to call home."

---

## THE ASK (1:35 - 2:00)

**[SCENE: Wayne speaking directly to camera. Sincere, determined expression.]**

**WAYNE:**
> "We're here at the Tim Draper Utah Entrepreneur Challenge because we believe Utah is the perfect place to build this company. The Lassonde Entrepreneur Institute has already been instrumental in our journey, and we're ready to scale."

**[SCENE: Show UEC prize money allocation graphic.]**

**WAYNE (V.O.):**
> "With the UEC prize, we'll invest in three critical areas: legal infrastructure to protect our students, marketing to reach every international student at UofU before Fall 2026, and technology to expand our roommate matching algorithm."

**[SCENE: Final shot of Wayne, warm smile, BridgeStay logo visible behind him.]**

**WAYNE:**
> "Two years ago, I was a scared international student with nowhere to live. Today, I'm building the platform I wish I had. Help us make sure no student ever has to feel that way again."

**[SCENE: BridgeStay logo with tagline: "Your Bridge to Home."]**

**WAYNE (V.O.):**
> "BridgeStay. Your bridge to home."

**[FADE TO BLACK]**

## PRODUCTION NOTES

**Visual Style:**
- Clean, modern aesthetic matching BridgeStay brand (warm oranges, ambers, whites)
- Mix of direct-to-camera and B-roll footage
- Animated graphics for statistics and platform demos
- Subtitles throughout for accessibility

**Music:**
- Opening: Soft, contemplative piano (0:00-0:15)
- Problem section: Subtle tension, building (0:15-0:45)
- Solution section: Upbeat, hopeful, inspiring (0:45-1:35)
- Closing: Triumphant, memorable (1:35-2:00)

**Key Shots Needed:**
1. Airport terminal scene (can be staged or stock footage)
2. Wayne speaking to camera (multiple angles)
3. UofU campus B-roll with international students
4. Screen recordings of BridgeStay platform
5. Animated infographics for statistics
6. Lassonde Studios exterior/interior

**Recommended Equipment:**
- 4K camera with good low-light performance
- Lavalier microphone for clear audio
- Softbox lighting for interview segments
- Gimbal for smooth B-roll footage
